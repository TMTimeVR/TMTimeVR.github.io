Introduction

At TMTimeStudios, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information in the course of our business, including through our VR game "Monkey Run VR".

Information We Collect

PlayFab

When you use "Monkey Run VR", PlayFab collects the following data:

  • Player Identifiers: PlayFab may collect unique identifiers, such as your PlayFab Player ID, which are used to provide you with a personalized gaming experience and track your progress.
  • Gameplay Data: PlayFab may collect gameplay data, including scores, achievements, and in-game actions, to enhance your gaming experience and improve the game.
  • Device Information: PlayFab may collect information about your device, such as its type, operating system, and hardware specifications, to ensure compatibility and optimize performance.

Photon

When you use "Monkey Run VR", Photon collects the following data:

  • Network Data: Photon collects data related to your network connection to enable multiplayer gameplay, including your IP address and connection quality.
  • Chat and Communication: Photon may collect chat messages and communication data during multiplayer sessions to facilitate in-game interactions.

How We Use User Data

PlayFab

We use PlayFab data in the following ways:

  • Personalization: Player identifiers are used to provide you with a personalized gaming experience, including tracking progress, achievements, and leaderboard rankings.
  • Improvements: Gameplay data helps us analyze and improve the game's features, difficulty levels, and overall user experience.

Photon

We use Photon data in the following ways:

  • Multiplayer Functionality: Photon data is essential for enabling multiplayer functionality in "Monkey Run VR", allowing you to play with other users around the world.
  • Gameplay Enhancements: Data collected during multiplayer sessions helps us optimize network performance and enhance the overall multiplayer gaming experience.
